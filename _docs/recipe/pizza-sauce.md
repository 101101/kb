# pizza-sauce  

> Simple pizza sauce recipe for NY style pizza.  


---

### Ingredients:  
20 oz can of crushed tomatoes  
1 1/2 tsp salt  
2 tsp oregano  
3 tsp basil  
1 tsp suger  

#### Optional ingredients:  
1 tsp crushed red pepper  

---

### Instructions:  
  1. Mix all ingredients in the can of crushed tomatoes  

#### Usage -  
  1. Use approximately 1/2 cup of mixed sauce per 16" pizza
